08 / Document

Paper stage

The document is always on warm paper — the dark stage behind it holds the page like a portfolio sleeve. Paper shadow shifts heavier to sit convincingly on the deep background.

Aurélien Gauthier

Cover letter · March 2026 · for Framer

Why Framer, why now

After six years shaping research tools at Stripe, I write the way I design: with the reader's breath in mind. Your recent work on spatial canvases reads as an invitation to rethink what a layout can be — and I'd like to help with that next turn.

What I'd bring

A habit of pairing with engineers on the first morning. A bias for quieter chrome and louder content. And a folder of small test files that usually becomes the basis of a shared system within a month.

What I'm hoping for

A thirty-minute conversation this week or next. I'll bring two specific questions about the motion system and leave with a clear sense of whether the fit is honest on both sides.
